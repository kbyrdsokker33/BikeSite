Who are we?

SwatWheels is a scooter rental service at Swarthmore College, consisting of a fleet of sleek and durable scooters that allow students to commute to class, team practice, the Matchbox or Fieldhouse, and travel cost efficiently to off-campus locations and more. Our scooters are designed with a heavy aluminum frame, a rear fender brake, big wheels and pneumatic tires made specifically for tackling any terrain. SwatWheels provides energy-efficient means of transportation, reduced travel time to any destination, and the comfort of no longer having to walk.


For questions or further inquiry, email us at swarthmorewheels@gmail.com or call at 678-575-2681.
